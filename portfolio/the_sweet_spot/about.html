About Us:

  

From the bottom of our hearts we would like to thank all our supporters for being there every step of the way. The Sweet Spot has the best chefs and bakers in the world. We have a test that only the best of the best can pass. Because of our amazing chefs and bakers, we were able to expand from New Jersey to not only across the country, but also around the world. We have a dish for everyone, whether you're a vegan, vegetarian, non-vegitarian, a sweet-lover, ect., we'll be sure to have you covered.
