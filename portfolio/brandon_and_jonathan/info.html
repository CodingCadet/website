DA MLG CAFE, ABOUT & HISTORY!!!

FOUNDED IN 1999, DA MLG CAFE CHAIN BEGAN IN THE SMALL CITY OF CYPRESS IN CALIFORNIA. THE FOUNDERS WERE BRANDON AND JONATHAN. BASING THEIR CAFE OFF OF MLG, MANY MLG PLAYERS THROUGHT THE WORLD CAME TO EAT HERE. TODAY, MLG CAFE IS ONE OF THE BIGGEST RESTAURANTS IN THE WORLD!!!
