History

Our first branch was opened in 1872 by Sally T. Kurra and Jenny R. Loi. Ever since, the number of branches have increased in the 143 years of business. Currently, the resturant and its fellow branches are owned by Mona R. Kurra and Emily K. Loi, 4th generation. "Sometimes are good times, but I have to travel around the world to check on my other branches." Mona quotes. She is in India visiting her family while her coworker, Emily is in charge until Mona gets back.
