About Us

Alice T. Kabelo

Hello! My name is Alice T. Kabelo and I opened ATK'S Bakery in 1950. This bakery is a family-run business. ATK'S Bakery has been running for over a decade and there are over 50 franchises in the United States. I love sweets such as strawberry velvet cupcakes and thats the reason I sell it. All the sweets I sell here are healthy and delicious! Now, I know you all want to know about me, so I'll stop rambling on about how fantastic my bakery is. My family and I travelled all the way from Germany to the United States. My siblings Katherine and Luke built the bakery brick by brick with the help of my parents. I opened the bakery in 1950 and we were awarded "#1 Bakery" in 1965, 1972, and 1981. We were also awarded "Best Place To Dine In" for 20 consecutive years.
