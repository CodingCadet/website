Tech

Coding Cadet utilizes a variety of technologies to enhance our student experience.

Cloud9

Think of Cloud9 as the Google Docs of code. The web application allows students to store all of their learning progress online. Cloud9 encourages students to continue their learning at home and enables student collaboration on projects.

We created a Cloud9 account for all of our students to login and store their web design workspaces. Students have access to all of their progress at home through a single login. Students are able to effectively reinforce their understanding of website design principles anywhere without the struggle of transferring files back and forth.

Students are also able to work on projects side by side using Cloud9. The beginner program ends with a final project, in which students create a website with a partner. Groups are able to collaborate on the website simultaneously, with changes made by one shown on the other's screen in real time. Cloud9 makes group web development much more effective.

Netlify

Typically, hosting even the simplest of websites requires setting up a server and DNS records, a job even the most passionate of students find boring and uninteresting. Netlify simplifies the task, boiling a multi-step procedure down to a few, easy to remember text commands.
