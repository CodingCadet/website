Student Websites

After 7 weeks of learning basic HTML and CSS, our students were tasked to build a simple website. Over the next 3 weeks, students worked in pairs in order to create a functional website utilizing all the tools they were taught. Though there were a number of hardships, every single student was able to ultimately complete the project. The official prompt was:
You have been hired by a new restaurant owner who is offering both a bakery and restaurant. The entrepreneur specializes in making baked goods from breads to muffins and pastries, as well as simple lunch or dinner menu items for the casual diner. Develop a company identity with a name that has a cohesive design structure to be used throughout the website.
